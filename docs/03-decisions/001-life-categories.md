# Decision-01: Life Categories vs RPG Attributes

## Status
Accepted

## Decision
LifeOS will use real-life categories as the primary structure.

## Reasoning
The purpose of LifeOS is to improve real-world outcomes.
Gamification should support progress without replacing reality.

## Consequences
- Projects naturally belong to life areas.
- Analytics can reveal where time and effort are invested.
- Future game mechanics can exist without changing the foundation.
