# Decision 004 – Campaigns as Optional Focus Modes

## Status

Accepted

---

## Decision

Campaigns are optional periods of intentional focus.

Users may group one or more projects into a campaign to create a temporary area of emphasis.

Campaigns are not required to use LifeOS.

---

## Problem

Life priorities naturally change.

Requiring campaigns would introduce unnecessary friction and reduce flexibility.

---

## Rationale

Campaigns create meaningful chapters within a user's life.

They provide context for reflection, celebration, and review while allowing users to choose their own pace, duration, and intensity.

---

## Trade-offs

Pros

* Highly flexible.
* Supports changing priorities.
* Creates natural review moments.

Cons

* Adds another organizational layer.
* Some users may never use campaigns.

---

## Consequences

Projects should always exist independently of campaigns.

Campaigns should enhance existing work rather than replace the core project structure.

Campaign Reviews become the primary reflection mechanism within LifeOS.
