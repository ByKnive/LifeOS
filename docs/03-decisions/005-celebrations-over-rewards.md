# Decision 005 – Celebrations Over Rewards

## Status

Accepted

---

## Decision

LifeOS emphasizes celebrations rather than transactional rewards.

Achievements should encourage users to acknowledge meaningful progress rather than perform tasks for external incentives.

---

## Problem

Traditional reward systems can shift motivation from intrinsic enjoyment to external compensation.

---

## Rationale

Celebrations reinforce personal accomplishment.

A celebration may include a reward, but the purpose is reflection and recognition rather than payment.

---

## Trade-offs

Pros

* Encourages intrinsic motivation.
* Creates memorable milestones.
* Supports long-term engagement.

Cons

* Less immediately motivating for some users.
* Requires thoughtful achievement design.

---

## Consequences

Future achievements should trigger celebrations instead of simply awarding points.

Celebrations may include personal traditions such as experiences, small purchases, or social events.
