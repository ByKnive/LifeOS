# Decision 007 – Momentum Over Perfection

## Status

Accepted

---

## Decision

LifeOS prioritizes sustainable momentum rather than perfect consistency.

Missing days, changing priorities, or taking breaks should never be treated as failure.

The system should encourage users to resume progress whenever they are ready.

---

## Problem

Streak-based systems often punish normal life events by resetting progress.

This can reduce motivation and encourage users to abandon the system entirely.

---

## Rationale

Personal growth is rarely linear.

Momentum better reflects how people naturally learn, recover, and adapt over time.

---

## Trade-offs

Pros

* Reduces guilt.
* Encourages long-term engagement.
* Supports healthy expectations.

Cons

* Less competitive than streak systems.
* Requires alternative progress indicators.

---

## Consequences

LifeOS should avoid mechanics that punish breaks.

Future analytics should measure trends, consistency, and recovery rather than uninterrupted streaks.
